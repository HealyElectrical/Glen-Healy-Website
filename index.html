About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineering student at BCIT with practical experience across circuit design, embedded systems, sensor integration, and control. I enjoy taking projects end-to-end: defining requirements, designing schematics and PCBs in KiCad, building mechanical parts in CAD (FreeCAD, SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor), and validating systems with real measurements. I'm currently working on a smart mailbox capstone project that I’m aiming to turn into a business. The system is designed with real-world reliability in mind, including a Raspberry Pi Pico 2 W microcontroller that can perform redundant, must-work actions alongside the main processor. I’m especially drawn to building small robots and automation projects that blend electronics, software, and 3D design. Longer-term, I’m also interested in renewable energy projects and electric transportation systems, from micro e-transit to full EV platforms.
